## üí∞ Singapore Tax Benefits

### Corporate Tax
- **Standard Rate:** 17%
- **Startup Tax Exemption:** First 3 years, first $100K profit at 0%, next $200K at 8.5%
- **R&D Tax Deductions:** Up to 250% deduction on R&D expenses

### Tax Incentives
- **PIC Scheme:** Productivity and Innovation Credit (discontinued but legacy benefits)
- **EDB Grants:** Enterprise Development Board grants for tech companies
- **R&D Tax Incentives:** Enhanced deductions for R&D

### Example Tax Savings
- Year 1-3: $0 tax on first $100K profit
- R&D expenses: 250% deduction
- Effective tax rate: ~10-12% (with incentives)
