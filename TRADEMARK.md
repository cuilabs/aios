# AIOS Trademark Guidelines

**CUI Labs (Pte.) Ltd., Singapore - Trademark Usage Guidelines**

## Ownership

"AIOS" and the AIOS logo are trademarks of CUI Labs (Pte.) Ltd., Singapore ("CUI Labs"). These trademarks are protected by law and may not be used without permission.

## Permitted Uses

### 1. Referential Use

You may use the "AIOS" name to refer to the AIOS software in:
- Documentation
- Blog posts and articles
- Presentations
- Educational materials

**Requirements:**
- Use the name accurately
- Do not suggest endorsement by CUI Labs
- Include a trademark notice: "AIOS is a trademark of CUI Labs (Pte.) Ltd., Singapore"

### 2. Software Distribution

You may use the "AIOS" name when:
- Distributing unmodified AIOS software
- Referring to official AIOS releases
- Linking to the official AIOS repository

**Requirements:**
- Do not modify the software in ways that would mislead users
- Maintain all copyright and trademark notices
- Do not suggest your version is official unless it is

### 3. Community Projects

You may use the "AIOS" name for:
- Community forks (with proper attribution)
- Educational projects
- Non-commercial projects

**Requirements:**
- Clearly indicate if your project is not official
- Include disclaimer: "This is not an official CUI Labs project"
- Do not use the official AIOS logo without permission

## Prohibited Uses

### You May NOT:

1. **Use "AIOS" as your product name**
   - Do not name your product "AIOS" or "AIOS [Something]"
   - Use a different name for your product

2. **Use the AIOS logo without permission**
   - The logo is protected
   - Contact us for logo usage permissions

3. **Suggest official endorsement**
   - Do not imply CUI Labs endorses your product
   - Do not use "Official" or "Certified" without authorization

4. **Register "AIOS" as a trademark**
   - CUI Labs owns the trademark
   - Do not attempt to register similar marks

5. **Use in domain names**
   - Do not register domains like "aios.com" or "aios.org"
   - Use descriptive domains instead

## Forking and Distribution

### Forks

If you fork AIOS:
- You may use "AIOS" to refer to the software
- You must clearly indicate your fork is not official
- You must maintain all copyright and trademark notices
- You may not use "AIOS" as your product name

**Example:**
- ✅ "MyCompany's AIOS Fork"
- ✅ "AIOS - Custom Build"
- ❌ "MyCompany AIOS" (suggests official product)

### Distributions

If you distribute AIOS:
- You may use "AIOS" to refer to the software
- You must include all copyright and trademark notices
- You must not modify the software in misleading ways
